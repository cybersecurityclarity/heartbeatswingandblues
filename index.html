Our Story

What Is Heartbeat?

Heartbeat Swing & Blues is a swing dance community-oriented event built for one purpose: you.

It was imagined and designed as a way to connect dancers, create and grow friendships, and celebrate our mutual love for this dance. We created this event with affordability, accessibility, and attendee enjoyment as the focal part — because we believe extraordinary dance experiences shouldn't be out of reach.

The schedule was designed to maximise dance time, the comps are designed to emulate joy, and the entire event was made for one thing: for you, the attendee, to have an unforgettable time.

💃

Community First

Built by dancers, for dancers. Every decision prioritises connection and belonging over spectacle.

💙

Affordable & Accessible

We believe in making world-class dance events within reach for everyone in the community.

🎶

Maximum Dance Time

Everything is scheduled to keep you on the floor. Less waiting, more dancing.

🌏

Welcoming & Inclusive

A safe space built on respect, consent, and celebration of our shared love for movement.

Competitions

Our comps are designed with one goal: joy. Whether you're competing or watching, everyone's there to have the best time.

🕺

Lindy Hop M&M

Mix & Match prelims judged by instructors and guests. Finals go to the audience — voted live via QR code.

Prelims: 3 songs (medium → slow → fast), rotating partners. Looking for 6 leads, 6 follows.
Finals: All-skate open, then individual spotlight (1.5–2 min), all-skate close.
Rules: No aerials · No choreography · Have fun!
🎵

Balboa M&M

The same beloved format — special mix & match prelims, audience-voted finals. Pure Balboa joy.

Prelims: 3 songs (medium → slow → fast), rotating partners. Looking for 6 leads, 6 follows.
Finals: All-skate open, then individual spotlight (1.5–2 min), all-skate close.
Rules: No aerials · No choreography · Have fun!
🎶

Blues M&M

Bring the blues feeling. Prelims judged for connection and energy. Finals decided by your fellow dancers.

Prelims: 3 songs (medium → slow → fast), rotating partners. Looking for 6 leads, 6 follows.
Finals: All-skate open, then individual spotlight (1.5–2 min), all-skate close.
Rules: No aerials · No choreography · Have fun!
❤️

The People's Comp

Every night you'll receive a QR code to nominate 1 lead and 1 follow who gave you the best dance — not about skill level, but about the feeling. Comfort, energy, vibe — you choose.

🏆 Winners receive a free party pass to Heartbeat 2028!
🎧

DJ Battle

The best DJs from across Asia face off all weekend. Vote via QR code after each set. The top two Lindy Hop DJs meet in a head-to-head Sunday night finale — playing their absolute best to close the weekend!
